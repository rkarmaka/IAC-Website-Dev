Ranit Karmakar

Specialist Postdoc

Bio

Ranit is a Specialist Postdoc at the Image Analysis Collaboratory (IAC) at the Harvard Medical School. His research interests include classic medical image and data analysis using classical image analysis, machine learning, deep learning and predictive modeling. Prior to joining IAC, Ranit received his PhD in Computer Engineering from Michigan Technological University. His dissertation was focused on developing artificial algorithms for eye banking.
